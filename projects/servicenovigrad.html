Service Novigrad

This is an Android app that I built for a school project. I managed to familiarize myself with Android Studio using Java. The app replicates a big organization, like a city or a country and helps by offering services. Clients can login to see what are their available services (driving license, health card, etc.) and apply for one of them. Employees can approve requests, and manage the branch were they work. Finally, administrators have almost all of the rights, and can control pretty much everything there is.
Find the source code here: https://github.com/antonpp11/ServiceNovigrad
