005 : Ottleti App

Very recently, when the great club of Atletico de Madrid decided to create an affiliated club in my city, my friends and I decided we had to do something about it. We decided to create an app that would display news of Atletico Ottawa. It would allow people to have discussions, add comments, and have articles from multiple sources. I mainly worked on the backend, as well as the collection and organization of data.
Check out the app here: ottleti.com
